About Me

Hi, I’m Githmi Dasanayake, a second year Software Engineering undergraduate at the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka.

I see programming not just as writing code, but as a way of turning ideas into something real. Whether it’s a simple calculator or a small interactive program, I enjoy the process of building, breaking, and improving until everything works just right.

My journey in tech started with curiosity and that curiosity keeps pushing me to explore new tools, languages, and concepts. I’ve worked on mini Java projects like games and utilities, which have helped me develop a strong foundation in logic, problem-solving, and structured thinking.

What excites me most about software engineering is its power to create impact. I’m constantly learning, experimenting, and growing with the goal of becoming a developer who builds meaningful and user-friendly solutions.

Outside of coding, I enjoy discovering new ideas and challenging myself to think creatively, because I believe great developers don’t just write code, they think differently.
